Smart Connected and Equipped Greenhouse,
Science of Engineering Project, 2017

I worked on this project twice. We launched it with 3 classmates as part of the Accro Sciences program, on the proposal of our Science of Engineering teacher in the second year. Being very happy with the result, I took it up again the following year to make it my TPE subject (anticipated baccalaureate exam).
It is a greenhouse equipped with sensors and actuators that regulate its humidity, temperature, and light. The user can measure and adjust the internal environment of the greenhouse from a web interface.

Technologies Used

  • C, Arduino environment
  • Html, CSS (web interface)

Transversal Skills

  • Project management: Writing and monitoring a specification
  • Electronics, creation of control and power electrical circuits
  • SolidWorks design, machining of simple parts
  • Teamwork (three-person team)
  • Oral presentation, presentation at a fair
